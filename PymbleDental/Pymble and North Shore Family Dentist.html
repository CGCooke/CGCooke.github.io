Pymble Dental - Your Family Dentist on the North Shore

For over 83 years, Pymble Dental has been the family dentist of choice for residents on the North Shore. The reason for that goes beyond providing quality dental services – we help our patients keep their teeth for life. Being a family dentist means we treat several generations of the same family, from children just learning the importance of dental hygiene to grandparents requiring treatment to maintain their natural teeth or dental crown and bridge implants.

The relationship you have with your dentist shows in your smile – and that’s not simply a matter of their technical skill. At Pymble Dental, we truly care for and take a genuine personal interest in each and every one of our patients, many of whom have become our friends.

It's this dedication to our community that makes us a trusted North Shore dentist. Many patients have been coming to us continuously for 20 to 60 years. This is a testament to the quality of care we have always offered. We warmly welcome your family and friends to place similar confidence and trust in us.


Dental Care at Every Age


A child's first visit to the dentist will undoubtedly leave an impression. Therefore, it's our goal to make the experience positive and stress-free. Alleviating fear will help children see the dentist positively and encourage them in the maintenance of their teeth. If you’re currently looking for a children's dentist on the North Shore, rest assured that our Pymble dentistry staff are experienced not only in their professions, but as parents themselves. We understand the stresses that families face and will work to make your visit as comfortable and convenient as possible.


Gentle and Effective Dental Services for your Family


A wide range of services using the latest technologies makes Pymble Dental an easy choice for top quality dental services on the North Shore.

  • Preventive Dentistry – Ensure the whole family has healthy and trouble free teeth by having regular check-ups and cleanings.
  • General Dentistry – We can easily accommodate all your family's general dental needs from minor cavity fillings to more comprehensive diagnosis and treatment planning.
  • Children's Dentistry – Our team of experts will make sure young children learn the importance of dental hygiene in a relaxed and positive environment.
  • Cosmetic Dentistry – From reconstructing worn or broken teeth to correcting discolouration, we can give you back a youthful, healthy-looking smile.
  • Implant Dentistry – Accidents can happen to anyone, but dental implants can get you back on the road to looking and feeling like your former self.
  • Crowns and Bridges – When infection, decay or an accident affects the function of your teeth, dental crowns and bridges can give you back what you've lost;
  • Root Canal Treatment – Losing your teeth is traumatic. In cases of deep decay, a root canal treatment can often prevent the need for tooth extraction.
  • Emergency Dentistry – As the only North Shore dentist with an on staff registered dental technician, you can be assured of same-day care in case of an emergency with a denture.
